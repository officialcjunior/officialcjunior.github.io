About

Aswin

Hello, I’m Aswin.

I’m interested in understanding more about myself and the Universe so that I can improve it.

I consider myself to be a general enthusiast- and I have many interests. Here are some of them and what I like to work on and learn more about:

Computer science:

Currently, I’m a senior year Computer Science and Engineering undergraduate at Amrita University, Kollam. I’m a member of a student-run computer security club at my university- teambi0s- a club founded and mentored by Vipin Pavithran. At bi0s, I mentor students, manage the team, work on various projects and play CTFs. I’m also a member of Rizin- which is an Open Source organization that makes reverse engineering frameworks. I am into Open Source software and can be seen talking about computers, linux and everything else in its realm.

I like computers in general. I was introduced to reverse engineering and other low-level particulars at the beginning of my “computer journey” as a freshman at my university. But lately, as I got acquainted with other things of the stack, I consider myself a software developer in general, and I like almost all parts of the stack. I’m the most into operating systems- Linux, server administration, software design, intelligent systems and computer security.

Art

I like art and its aesthetics, especially: filmography, photography and writing. I put my writings on this blog, my everyday thoughts on Twitter, and my photos and bit of cinema on Instagram. I like silent and slow movies- the kind Alfonso Cuaron and Michael Haneke usually create.

I also love to play the piano and the guitar.

Life

I like rational discussions about life, the Universe, and the meaning and theory of everything. Therefore, I’m also into:

  • Philosophy: ideas for self-improvement.
  • Biology: how life and consciousness work.
  • Neuroscience: how the brain- thinking and memory- works and brain-computer interfaces.
  • Mathematics: how you can abstract many things around us- many things of the universe- with mathematical models.
  • Physics: elementary physics- about the fundamental structures of the universe.
